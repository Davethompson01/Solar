Delivering Alternative Energy Solutions

Our Story

One of the biggest challenges we face in Nigeria is the intermittency of power, the unreliable national grid and the energy deficit across the value chain. Nigerians have long used diesel generators and have gotten comfortable with them. UNAB global energy limited intends to solve this by accelerating the adoption of renewable energy solutions. Prioritising consumer education and providing resources that enable a seamless conversion that will help in creating brand awareness and product adoption will determine our success.
